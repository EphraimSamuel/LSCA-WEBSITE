About

Welcome to the official website of the Lagos State Cricket Association (LSCA) – your gateway to the exciting world of cricket in Lagos, Nigeria! Established with a vision to revolutionize and nurture the sport of cricket, LSCA has been at the forefront of promoting excellence, inclusivity, and development within the cricketing community.

Journey and Heritage: With a rich and storied history, the Lagos State Cricket Association traces its origins back to the passionate cricket enthusiasts who laid the foundation for the sport in the region. Over the years, we have evolved and grown into a dynamic organization that is deeply committed to elevating cricket to new heights. Our journey has been defined by dedication, collaboration, and an unwavering spirit to make cricket a sport that resonates with every individual in Lagos.

Empowering Cricketing Aspirations: At LSCA, we firmly believe that cricket transcends boundaries and fosters a sense of unity among diverse communities. Our primary goal is to empower cricketing aspirations and provide a platform for budding talent to shine. Through a comprehensive range of programs and initiatives, we aim to unearth the hidden potential of young cricketers, equipping them with the skills, knowledge, and values necessary to excel both on and off the field.

A Holistic Cricketing Experience: With a relentless focus on holistic development, LSCA offers a multifaceted cricketing experience that extends beyond the boundary ropes. From top-notch coaching sessions conducted by seasoned mentors to state-of-the-art infrastructure that nurtures a passion for the game, we leave no stone unturned in ensuring that cricket enthusiasts, regardless of their age or background, find a place to flourish within our cricketing ecosystem.

Championing Diversity and Inclusivity: In line with the spirit of Lagos – a city celebrated for its diversity and vibrancy – we champion inclusivity in every aspect of our operations. LSCA celebrates the participation of women and girls in cricket and endeavors to create an environment that fosters equal opportunities for all. We firmly believe that a diverse cricketing community enriches the sport and strengthens the bond among its followers.

Building Strong Foundations: Our commitment to building strong foundations is evident in our grassroots programs, which lay the groundwork for a bright future in cricket. Through strategic partnerships with schools and educational institutions, we introduce the sport to young talents, igniting their passion for cricket and nurturing their potential through a structured and supportive pathway.

Join the LSCA Family: Whether you are a seasoned cricketer seeking opportunities to elevate your game or a beginner eager to embark on a journey into the cricketing world, the Lagos State Cricket Association welcomes you with open arms. Our website serves as your comprehensive guide to the latest updates, fixtures, news, and events within the cricketing fraternity of Lagos.

Together, Let's Make History: As we march ahead on our journey to make cricket a sport that unites, inspires, and delights, we invite you to join hands with us and be a part of history in the making. Together, let's celebrate the joy of cricket, forge lasting friendships, and create a legacy that will stand the test of time. Thank you for visiting the Lagos State Cricket Association website. We hope your cricketing adventure with us is nothing short of extraordinary!
